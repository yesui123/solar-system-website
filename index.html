The Solar System

The Solar System is made up of the Sun, eight planets, their moons, and many smaller objects like dwarf planets, asteroids, and comets. The planets orbit the Sun in a predictable path called an orbit.

Order from the Sun: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune. Learn more on the Citations page.
