Gas & Ice Giants

The outer planets are Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. They are located far from the Sun and are much larger than the inner planets. These planets are mostly made of gas or ice, have many moons, and all have ring systems. They are cold and take a long time to orbit the Sun.

Jupiter

Largest planet. Famous for the Great Red Spot which is a huge storm. It has dozens of moons.

Saturn

Known for its bright and spectacular rings. A gas giant with a complex moon system. Saturn is very light or its size.

Uranus

An ice giant that spins on its side. It has a blue-green color due to methane gas.

Neptune

Farthest known planet from the Sun. It is an ice giant with very strong winds. Strong winds and a deep blue colour.
