What are “Inner” planets?

Inner planets are closer to the Sun and are small, dense, and rocky. They include Mercury, Venus, Earth, and Mars.

Mercury

Smallest planet and closest to the Sun. Extreme temperature swings. Surface covered in rocks and craters.

Venus

Similar size to Earth but has a thick, hot atmosphere. Surface pressure and temperature are very high.

Earth

Our home planet with liquid water on the surface and life. One moon and an active atmosphere.

Mars

Known as the Red Planet due to iron oxide. Has the largest volcano (Olympus Mons) and polar ice caps.
